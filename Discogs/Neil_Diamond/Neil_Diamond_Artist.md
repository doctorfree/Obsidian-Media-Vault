# Neil Diamond

![](../../assets/artists/Neil_Diamond.png)

## Artist Profile

American singer-songwriter and occasional actor, born January 24, 1941 in Brooklyn, New York, USA. Father of Jesse Diamond. Inducted into the Songwriters Hall of Fame in 1984.
