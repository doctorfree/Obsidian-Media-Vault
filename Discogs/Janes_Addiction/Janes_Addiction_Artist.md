# Jane's Addiction

![](../../assets/artists/Janes_Addiction.png)

## Artist Profile

American rock band founded in 1985 in Los Angeles.

Original line-up:
Perry Farrell: vocals
Dave Navarro: guitar
Eric Avery: bass
Stephen Perkins: drums
