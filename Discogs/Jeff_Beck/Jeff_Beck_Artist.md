# Jeff Beck

![](../../assets/artists/Jeff_Beck.png)

## Artist Profile

British guitarist.
Born: June 24, 1944 in Wallington, Surrey, United Kingdom.

Inducted into Rock And Roll Hall of Fame in 2009 (Performer).
