# Lisa Lisa & Cult Jam

![](../../assets/artists/Lisa_Lisa_and_Cult_Jam.png)

## Artist Profile

New York hip-hop/R&B group from the mid-1980s. The group consisted of singer Lisa Velez and musicians Mike Hughes and Alex "Spanador" Moseley.
