# Little Eva

![](../../asssets/artists/Little_Eva.png)

## Artist Profile

American pop singer, born 29 June 1943 in Bellhaven, North Carolina, USA and died 10 April 2003 in Kinston, North Carolina, USA.
Sister of Idalia Boyd.
