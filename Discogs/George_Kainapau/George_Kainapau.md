# George Kainapau

![](../../asssets/artists/George_Kainapau.png)

## Artist Profile

Born on May 22, 1905
Died on September 14, 1992
2010: Posthumous winner of a Nā Hōkū Hanohano Lifetime Achievement Award under the auspices of the Hawaiʻi Academy of Recording Arts.
