# Stonewall Jackson

![](../../assets/artists/Stonewall_Jackson.png)

## Artist Profile

American country singer/songwriter and musician born November 6, 1932 in Tabor City, North Carolina; died December 4, 2021. Brother of songwriter Wade Jackson (2).
