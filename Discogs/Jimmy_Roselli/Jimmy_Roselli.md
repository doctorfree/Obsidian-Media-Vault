# Jimmy Roselli

![](../../asssets/artists/Jimmy_Roselli.png)

## Artist Profile

Italian-American pop singer.

Born December 26th, 1925 in Hoboken, New Jersey. 
Died June 30th, 2011 at his house in Clearwater, Florida.
