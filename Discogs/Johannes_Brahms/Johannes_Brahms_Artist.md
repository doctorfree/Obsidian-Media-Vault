# Johannes Brahms

![](../../assets/artists/Johannes_Brahms.png)

## Artist Profile

Born: 1833-05-07 (Hamburg, Germany).
Died: 1897-04-03 (Vienna, Austria).

Johannes Brahms was a German composer, pianist, and conductor of the Romantic period.
