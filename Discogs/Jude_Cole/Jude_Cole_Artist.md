# Jude Cole

![](../../assets/artists/Jude_Cole.png)

## Artist Profile

Jude Cole is a manager, record producer and singer/songwriter/guitarist. Cole is manager of the American rock band, Lifehouse, and has co-written songs with Jason Wade.
