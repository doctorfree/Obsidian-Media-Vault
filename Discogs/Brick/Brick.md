# Brick

![](../../asssets/artists/Brick.png)

## Artist Profile

Soul funk disco quintet 

Formed mid 70's, in Atlanta, Georgia
Active between 1976 - 1988.
Their singles "Dusic" and "Dazz" are considered among the best of funk legacy.
