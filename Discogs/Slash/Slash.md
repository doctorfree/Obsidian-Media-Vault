# Slash

![](../../asssets/artists/Slash.png)

## Artist Profile

Born: July 23, 1965, London, England

Lead guitarist of Guns N' Roses, formerly a member of Velvet Revolver.

Previously worked at Tower Records in Sunset Boulevard.
