# Jimmy Durante

![](../../asssets/artists/Jimmy_Durante.png)

## Artist Profile

American singer, pianist, comedian, and actor.
Born February 10, 1893, New York City, New York, USA - died January 20, 1980, Santa Monica, California, USA.
