# Les Paul & Mary Ford

![](../../assets/artists/Les_Paul_and_Mary_Ford.png)

## Artist Profile

Popular 1950s husband-and-wife/group musical team in which Les Paul played the guitar and Mary Ford sang. In 1951 alone, they sold six million records.
