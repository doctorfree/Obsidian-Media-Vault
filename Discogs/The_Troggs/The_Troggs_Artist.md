# The Troggs

![](../../assets/artists/The_Troggs.png)

## Artist Profile

English rock band formed in Andover, Hampshire in 1964.

Reg Presley: Vocals & Ocarina
Chris Britton: Guitar
Peter Staples: Bass
Ronnie Bond: Drums
