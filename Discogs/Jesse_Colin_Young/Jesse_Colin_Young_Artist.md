# Jesse Colin Young

![](../../assets/artists/Jesse_Colin_Young.png)

## Artist Profile

American guitarist, bassist, singer, and songwriter. 

Born: 22 November 1941 in Queens, New York City, New York, USA. 

Founding member of the band The Youngbloods.
