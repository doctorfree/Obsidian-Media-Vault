# Steam

![](../../asssets/artists/Steam.png)

## Artist Profile

American pop group, known for the 1969 hit Na Na Hey Hey Kiss Him Goodbye.
The song was put together as a "B" side, & written & recorded by studio musicians who were yet unnamed .
