# Big Brother & The Holding Company

![](../../asssets/artists/Big_Brother_and_The_Holding_Company.png)

## Artist Profile

BluesRock Psychedelic band, formed 1965 in San Francisco, California, USA.

Janis Joplin - vocals
James Gurley - guitar
Sam Andrew - guitar
Peter Albin - bass
Dave Getz - drums
