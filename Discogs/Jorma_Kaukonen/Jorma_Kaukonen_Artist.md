# Jorma Kaukonen

![](../../assets/artists/Jorma_Kaukonen.png)

## Artist Profile

American guitarist, singer and songwriter, born December 23, 1940 in Washington, D.C. Founding member of Jefferson Airplane and Hot Tuna.
Older brother of Peter Kaukonen
